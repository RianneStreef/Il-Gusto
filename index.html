Welcome

Welcome to the 4 stars residence l’Oxalys at the entrance of Val Thorens. Our new and dynamic team will great you and help you discover our asian food proposed by our Japanese chef and also local Savoie dishes highly recommended and tasteful in a warm and comfortable atmosphere. Enjoy our new menu, tasty, colorful and exotic. And don’t forget to try our delicious pizza’s culinary delights to please every plate. More over, enjoy your lunch with wonderful views from our southward looking outdoor terrace using the “Oxalys chair lift” or “Cairn cable car”. You can reach us by “Pluviometre” ski slope if you arrive from 3 Valley. Or by walking through the entrance of “Oxalys résidence”. Our team is pleased to welcome you to share an enjoyable moment.
